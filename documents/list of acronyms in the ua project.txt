list of acronyms used in the ua project:
===================================================

UA . . . . . . universe algorithm.

UAJ . . . . . universe algorithm video journal (UA journal)

iter . . . . . itenerary, list of things to talk about in a UAJ

1 . . . . . . pointer (i) increment-by-1 operation          i++;

2 . . . . . . comparator (*n) increment-by-1 operation          (*n)++;

3 . . . . . . modnat (*i, deferenced pointer) increment-by-1 operation     (*i)++;

5 . . . . . . pointer (i) set-to-zero operation            i = 0;

6 . . . . . . comparator (*n) set-to-zero operation        *n = 0;

7 . . . . . . modnat (*i, deferenced pointer) set-to-zero operation       *i = 0;

9 . . . . . . equal branch between i and n

B . . . . . . equal branch between *i and m

C . . . . . . equal branch between *n and *i         if (*n == *i) goto ... else goto ...

E . . . . . . less than branch between *n and *i      if (*n < *i) goto ... else goto ...

F . . . . . . greater than branch between *n and *i      if (*n > *i) goto ... else goto ...

N . . . . . . numerus, second parameter to UA, one less than the number of cells in the UA array. (location of last cell)

M . . . . . . modulus, first parameter to UA, one less than the number of states per cell. (value of largest cell state)

ISA . . . . . instruction set architecture (instruction set)

RHS . . . . . right hand side

LHS . . . . . left hand side

LRS . . . . . least residue system (e.g., the LRS(5) is the set {0, 1, 2, 3, 4}.)

c . . . . . . characteristic of a computational system

CFG . . . . . control flow graph, (a piece of code)

XFG . . . . . (m=infinity,n=infinity) XP-formulation CFG

XP . . . . . . expansion pattern

XPC . . . . . expansion pattern check PM

R . . . . . . RRXFG

RRXFG . . . . reduced reduced XFG

RXFG . . . . . reduced XFG

NF . . . . . . nested for-loop algorithm 

SRNFGPR . . . . segmented-range  nested for-loop  generate-pruned  with RMV

IP . . . . . . iterative pruning utility

MTRC . . . . . mode trichotomy correspondence

RMTRC . . . . . revised mode trichotomy correspondence

COTB . . . . . combined operation ternary-branch instruction model

ERW . . . . . ER walk PM

XW . . . . . expansion width

PRT . . . . . pre-run timestep count

EL . . . . . execution limit (instruction count)

LE . . . . . leading expansion edge

PPR . . . . . positional unary pointer register

CMC . . . . . cardinal unary memory cell

PCO . . . . pointer COI optimization constraint/principle

COI . . . . change of information  over time

ZR5 . . . . zero resets using 5 principle

ZR6 . . . . zero resets using 6 principle

H . . . . . horizontal line PM

H1 . . . . . horizontal line 1 PM

H2 . . . . . horizontal line 2 PM

FEA . . . . finite end of the array PM

PM . . . . . pruning metric

NSVLPM . . . . nonsimplified vertical line PM

SVL . . . . . simplified vertical line PM

NS0 . . . . no-skip over zero modnats PM

R0I . . . . repetitive *0 increment PM

RMV . . . . repeated modnat value PM

DOL . . . . duplicate operation list

NDH . . . . . nested DOL hypothesis

ZV . . . . . Z value

ZI . . . . . Z index

Z . . . . . CFG definition

D . . . . . duplicate operation count, aka duplication count

0-sp . . . . D = 0 search space

1-sp . . . . D = 1 search space

2-sp . . . . D = 2 search space

3-sp . . . . D = 3 search space

CSM . . . . consecutive small modnat PM

OT . . . . . operation timeout PM

SG . . . . . synthesize graph utility

viz . . . . visualization utility

DT . . . . . date time (eg, 1202405083.161656)

TB . . . . . thought bin document

BDL . . . . . backwards diagonal line

BDL2 . . . . . backwards diagonal line 2

OER . . . . . oscilatory ER

RER . . . . . repetitive ER

ER . . . . . . early return 

OC . . . . . . operation count

IA . . . . . . increment and advance

MCAL . . . . . . modnat COI abstract lifetime PM

NDI . . . . . . no double incrementing principle

F1E . . . . . . first 1 expansion (*0 = 1) PM

ERC . . . . . . ER required count PM

MM . . . . . .  modnat at maximum PM

SNM . . . . . . *n at maximum PM

SNL . . . . . . *n infinite loop PM

GA . . . . . .  graph analysis pruning stage

VL . . . . . . vertical line

VLI . . . . . . VL inverse precession speed

TS . . . . . . timesteps

COI-LE-TS . . . displaying the state of the array every time 5 is executed.

COI-TS . . . displaying the state of the array on every instruction that is executed.

MPP . . . . middle portion percentage

MFEA . . . . multi-FEA PM

BT . . . . . backtrack

BLT . . . . branch if less than

BNE . . . . branch if not equal

BEQ . . . . branch if equal

BNZ . . . . branch if non-zero

BZ . . . . branch if zero

BGE . . . . branch if greater than or equal

PER . . . . pointless ER PM  (formerly parsimonious COI constraint PM)

36R . . . . . RRXFG which has 3 going unconditionally to 6   (R = 0)

63R . . . . . RRXFG which has 6 going unconditionally to 3   (R = 1)

FDL . . . . . forward diagonal line PM

PA . . . . . physical address in a graph

EDA . . . . empirical deadstop analysis PM

De-Dup . . . de-duplication of lifetime's of z values, ie uniquifying the z list.
